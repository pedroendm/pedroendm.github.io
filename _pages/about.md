### Hello, there!

I am a PhD student in Computer Science, jointly supervised by **[Prof. Luís Paulo Reis](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=bCKD3moAAAAJ) from the University of Porto** and **[Prof. Herke van Hoof](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=9owUkLYAAAAJ) from the University of Amsterdam**.

I am interested in bridging the gap between AI planning and reinforcement learning. My research interests include reinforcement learning, planning, representation learning, and neuro-symbolic AI, with a focus on building generalizable, explainable, and trustworthy agents that can operate in both simulated and physical environments. My long-term goal is to develop generally capable AI agents that can reason, learn, and adapt across tasks and environments.

I am also a member of **FCPortugal’s RoboCup 3D Simulation League team** since 2022. We are four-time world champions. I also served on the **RoboCup 3D Simulation League Technical Committee** from 2023 to 2024, and I have been an **Invited Teaching Assistant at the University of Porto** since 2023.

I hold a **BSc and MSc in Computer Science from the University of Porto**, graduating top of my class in both degrees. See my CV for more information.
